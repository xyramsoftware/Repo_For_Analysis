Our Purpose

 The main objective of the internal sport festival of kerala is to serve as a distraction to all the uprise of illegal practise and actives that are blinding our youth from a much bigger purpose. We are targeting to bring a campaign forward against the amount of rising drug addiction in city as a whole and to try and bring it to end. Another idea behind the international sports festival of kerala is to bring out the youth performance and also witness the amazing talents in sports, wellness and entertainment that their peers process. We also aim to bring out a new sports culture to the people of kochi and to bring all of it’s resident out of witness the sports spirit of kochi in all of it’s glory.
